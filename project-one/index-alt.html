POIRET ONE

An Art Deco Typeface

This sleek typeface was designed by Denis Masharov. Designed for versatility in advertising, Poiret One belongs to the geometric grotesque family. Its light strokes and elegant curves reflect the art deco period of the 1920s, while the slanted crossbar in the e reflects a touch of the older humanist style of type, making this typeface an elegant classic.
