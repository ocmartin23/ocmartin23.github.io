Dame's Rocket

Hesperis matronalis

Dame's Rocket blooms in colorful swaths of purple, pink, and white. The scent of the flowers is clearest at the end of the day, hence the root word for the genus â€“ Hesperis comes from the Greek hesperos , meaning evening.

Viewing Note

For a gorgeous display of Dame's Rocket in bloom, visit Shenandoah River State Park in Bentonville, VA. Follow the Bluebell Trail to the River Trail. Dame's Rocket blooms plentifully along the riverbank from April to August.
