Hey y'all! I'm Olivia Martin (or Liv, if you prefer), and my pronouns are she/they. I'm a class of '23 mathematics major at Goshen College, with a minor in graphic design and special interests in statistical analysis, data visualization, and education research and policy.


Fun Facts

  • The best day of my life was the day I saw a black bear in Shenandoah National Park. The greatest regret of my life is that I was driving and couldn't take a picture.
  • I own a growing collection of crowns and tiaras, which I wear on special occasions and around the house for fun and emotional support.
  • A few years ago I saw people online making earrings from worms on a string. I decided to try it out, but things got out of hand and now I'm the Worm Queen. I made crown jewels and a worm gown.
  • I love the eldritch horror genre (think Old Gods of Appalachia, The Magnus Archives, and H.P. Lovecraft) but I can't stand horror movies and video games.
  • My favorite musical artists are Hozier and Lord Huron. My favorite visual artists are Vincent Van Gogh, Ingo Swann, and Bianca Roman-Stumpff.
